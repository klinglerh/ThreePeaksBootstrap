What we do

Three Peaks Lodge is a 100 acre property nestled in the small town of Saint Anthony Idaho. In 2003 we opened our doors and quickly became a favorite reunion destination for hundreds of families. As time has passed we have continued to improve our home and surrounding property to make it as welcoming, enjoyable, and fun as possible. Our square foot home can comfortably host people for special events and for those looking to book for a reunion or overnight stay we are able to sleep up to people.
